Google Map


Setup Your Location
you need latitude and longitude of your location. you can try this site http://itouchmap.com/latlong.html

check the code

pop-uptext
  1. <!-- map -->
  2. <div class="map">
  3.   <div class="collapse" id="map-toogle">
  4.     <div class="map-area ">
  5.       <div class="sh-top"></div>
  6.       <!-- change data-lat="48.856318" data-lng="2.351866" with your location-->
  7.       <div class="map" id="map" data-lat="48.856318" data-lng="2.351866"></div>
  8.       <div class="map-scroll-space"></div>
  9.       <div class="sh-bottom"></div>
  10.     </div>
  11.   </div>
  13.   <div class="btn-map" >
  14.     <div class="container text-center">
  15.       <a href="#map-toogle" class="font-alt" data-toggle="collapse" >
  16.         <span class="ic-map" >
  17.           <i class="fa fa-map-marker"></i>
  18.         </span>
  19.         View Map
  20.       </a>
  21.     </div>
  22.   </div>
  23. </div><!-- end map -->
